Introduction

Power is supplied to domesticate installations through a phase and neutral, forming a single phase AC 230v to wire system. For individual establishment power is supplied through three plase two wire system. To give 440v , the neutral is earthed at to the domestic utilities; power is fed to kilo watt meter and then to distributes power along several circuits. It also protects these circuits from overload by safety devices like fuses or circuit breakers.

Staircase Wiring

Staircase wiring, also known as two-way switching, enables control of a light from two separate positions, such as the top and bottom of a staircase or beside a bed and door. This setup is particularly useful for turning off a light after moving between these locations, enhancing both convenience and safety. It is commonly used in homes and buildings to improve energy efficiency and provide ease of access.

Two lamps connected in series and one in parallel

Two lamps were connected in series, and one lamp was connected in parallel, all controlled by a single switch. After drawing and approving the circuit diagram, the wiring was completed with the positive terminal connecting to the switch and series lamps, and the negative terminal connecting to the parallel lamp. Upon applying AC power, all lamps lit up, confirming the successful wiring and control with one switch.
